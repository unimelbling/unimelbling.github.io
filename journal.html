Nuanced Garbling

Nuanced Garbling is an annual undergraduate journal that aims to provide an opportunity for students of linguistics at the University of Melbourne to share the results of their studies and add to the collective knowledge of the student body. Students are encouraged to submit original papers on any linguistics subdiscipline or linguistics aligned disciplines, written either for university subjects or out of personal interest.

To submit a paper for Nuanced Garbling 2021, please contact us via email or message our Facebook page .
